About NIL Impact

NIL Impact is the first platform where college student athletes can securely share and compare their Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) compensation data. Our mission is to create transparency in the NIL landscape, helping athletes make informed decisions about their opportunities.

How It Works

  1. Sign Up: Create an account with your college email address
  2. Verify: Confirm your student athlete status
  3. Explore: Browse compensation data from other athletes
  4. Share: Submit your own NIL deals (optional)
  5. Compare: Use our tools to compare deals across conferences and sports
