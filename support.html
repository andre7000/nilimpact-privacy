Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use NIL Impact?

NIL Impact is designed for current college student athletes to share their NIL compensation experiences.

Do I need a college email to sign up?

Yes, we require a verified college (.edu) email address to ensure authenticity of submitted data.

Is my data private?

Yes, you can choose to post anonymously or with a display name. Personal information is never shared.

How do I submit compensation data?

After creating your account, use the "Add Comp" tab to submit your NIL deals and compensation information.
